Hi, my name is _____ and I am Petco customer.

I don't want Petco to use Goldman Sachs for its stock offering. Goldman Sachs has a record of cheating and bankrupting its clients, and I don't want this to happen to Petco.

Please call me back at [your phone number] and tell me how you are going to respond to my concerns about Goldman Sachs.

Thank you for your time.

Tips
  • Be friendly and polite - the person picking up the phone will find it hard to ignore you if you are polite.
  • Keep it short.
  • Don’t feel like you have to answer any specific questions if you don’t feel comfortable.
