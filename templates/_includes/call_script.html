Hi my name is ________

Can I please speak to Minister Greg Hunt? (They will probably say that you should leave him a message)

I’m calling Minister Hunt to tell him that I’m someone who cares deeply about our Great Barrier Reef, and so I’m asking him to not re-approve the Carmichael coal mine.

The mine is damaging to our climate and will ruin our Great Barrier Reef. Can you pass this message to Minister Hunt please?

Thank you for your time.

Tips
  • Be friendly and polite - the person picking up the phone will find it hard to ignore you if you are polite.
  • Keep it short.
  • Don’t feel like you have to answer any specific questions if you don’t feel comfortable.
